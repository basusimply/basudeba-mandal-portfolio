## Why journeys matter

Availability of a **component** is not the same as **user success**. Map critical paths—login, checkout, data sync—and measure what breaks the experience.

## Error budgets in practice

When budget burns, **freeze features** and **fund reliability**. When budget is healthy, you can take more product risk.

## Tools are secondary

Prometheus, Grafana, and good **on-call culture** help—but **clarity on what “good” means** comes first.
