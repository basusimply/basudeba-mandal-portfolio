## The usual suspects

Idle clusters, oversized VMs, and environments left running **24/7** for “just in case.”

## A playbook

1. **Tag and attribute** cost so teams see their footprint.
2. **Automate scale-down** outside business hours where safe.
3. **Review** quarterly with engineering leads—not only finance.

## Mindset

Cost optimization is **continuous**—like security and reliability—not a one-time project.
